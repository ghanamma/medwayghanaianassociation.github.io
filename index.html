Medway Ghanaian Association

Welcome

The MEDWAY GHANAIAN ASSOCIATION is a non-political, non-profitable, non-denominational union for Ghanaians living in Medway, UK.

Our Objectives

  • Promote health and wellbeing
  • Establish a social network of Ghanaians where we entertain, educate, and inform them about government policies
  • We sign post and enable public and private sector organisations to have access to the Ghanaian community in Medway
